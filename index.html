SPA

Marvel Comics is considered the largest comic book publisher in the world. Since the late 1930s, Marvel has enchanted people with its stories.

The Marvel Comics API allows developers anywhere to access information about a vast library of Marvel comics - from what's to come until 70 years ago.

This SPA shows the selection of three Marvel Hero Teams (X-Men, Avengers and Guardians of the Galaxy). By clicking on each of the images referring to each of these teams, the description of the Team, the listing of one of the heroes and a button (non-functional) to show other heroes is shown.
